🔒 Data privacy

Your keys are private and stay on your device. Every photo, fingerprint, label, and category is stored only in this browser's local storage on this phone — never uploaded to any server, and Key Buddy has no account or cloud. Your data leaves this device only through an export file that you choose to share (see Sync above). The app makes no network calls except to load its image-recognition libraries once, which are then cached for offline use.

Use the button below to permanently erase every key and fingerprint from this device. This cannot be undone.

Settings

Auto-scan (Identify)

When on, the Identify screen watches the live camera and matches keys continuously — no button press. When off, you tap a button to capture.

Auto-scan uses more battery than tap-to-capture; pausing conserves it. Resume anytime by tapping the camera or the Resume button.
